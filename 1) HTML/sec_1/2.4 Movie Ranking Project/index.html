The Best of David Lynch

My top 3

1. Lost Highway (1997)

Fred Madison, a jazz saxophonist, shares an affectionate relationship with his wife, Renee.
However, his life changes when he receives a threatening message from a mysterious man.

2.Mulholland Drive(2001)

Rita, a dark-haired amnesiac, and Betty, a perky blonde actress,
team up to find clues related to Rita's accident and ascertain her true identity.

3.Twin Peaks(1990)

Dale Cooper, an eccentric FBI agent, visits Twin Peaks, a quaint little town,
to investigate the murder of Laura Palmer, a 17-year-old school girl.
